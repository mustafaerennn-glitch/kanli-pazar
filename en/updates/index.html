Chapter 2 — Provocative Distortion in the Tümtürk Quote Corrected Correction
10 May 2026 · Chapter 2
When the İsmet Tümtürk quotation, sourced from Kovara Bir, No. 5, was compared against the original source, Milli Yol, No. 13 (20 April 1967), it was found that the secondary source had made a provocative addition. The text has been revised to stay faithful to the original source.
The Claim of Eygi's Special Warfare Connection, and His Response Note
8 May 2026 · Chapter 3
Mehmet Şevket Eygi, chief columnist of the newspaper Bugün, is one of the figures examined in detail in this chapter of the book. About fifty years after Kanlı Pazar, Eygi made headlines again over a different claim. Retired Lieutenant General İsmail Hakkı Pekin stated in 2018 that Eygi had been assigned within the Special Warfare Department in 1959. Eygi rejected the claims in seven separate columns in Milli Gazete. The debate also spread to Akit TV, where Pekin reiterated that his claim was backed by documentation.
1946: The Sixth Fleet in Turkish Territorial Waters Text
3 May 2026 · Introduction
New content has been added to the Introduction chapter concerning 1946, the year of the Sixth Fleet's first arrival in Turkey. Built around the arrival in Istanbul of the USS Missouri, part of the Sixth Fleet, this addition points to an earlier historical backdrop for the process examined in the work. The text has been incorporated into the chapter from an article featured in the anthology Türkiye'nin 68'i: Denizlere Çıkan Sokaklar [Turkey's '68: Streets Flowing to the Seas], published by the Istanbul Metropolitan Municipality in 2022.
